About Fashion Tagger

Fashion Tagger is an AI-powered application designed to help you identify and label various fashion items in your images. Whether you're a fashion enthusiast, a blogger, or someone who loves keeping track of fashion trends, our application provides detailed and accurate labels for your fashion images.

Using state-of-the-art machine learning models, Fashion Tagger can identify different categories, types, colors, and other attributes of fashion items, making it easier for you to organize and understand your fashion collection.

Dataset reference: Fashion Product Images Dataset
